The U Greats!

It's all about the "U"...

The Miami Hurricanes are among the most storied and decorated football programs in NCAA history. Miami also holds a number of NFL Draft records, including most first-round selections in a single draft and most consecutive drafts with at least one first-round selection. The University of Miami has a rich history of great football players that have gone on to have hall of fame Collegiate and Professional careers. The program has won five AP national championships (1983, 1987, 1989, 1991, 2001).
